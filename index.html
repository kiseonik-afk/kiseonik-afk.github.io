NOVO U PONUDI

CHANEL No5

Light Blue by Dolce&Gabbana is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. Light Blue was launched in 2001. The nose behind this fragrance is Olivier Cresp. Top notes are Sicilian Lemon, Apple, Cedar and Bellflower; middle notes are Bamboo, Jasmine and White Rose; base notes are Cedar, Musk and Amber.

Very arid and acidic lemon, more peel than pulp. Fresh acidic green apple, with a touch of cedar and musk to balance things out. The impression is very much a sundried lemon tree on a beach; parched fruit and leaves, dried out husk of a trunk with brittle branches, with a persisting scent of the sea. It's quite clever, but the effect is often lost since it's so commercially popular.
